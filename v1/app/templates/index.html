How it Works

User-Based Collaborative Filtering

Recommends items based on preferences of similar users. The system identifies users with similar taste patterns and recommends items they enjoyed but you haven't tried yet.

Item-Based Collaborative Filtering

Recommends items similar to ones you've already liked. The system analyzes relationships between items based on user ratings to find items that are frequently liked together.

Popularity-Based Recommendations

Recommends the most popular items based on overall ratings. This approach is particularly useful for new users with limited history (cold start problem).
